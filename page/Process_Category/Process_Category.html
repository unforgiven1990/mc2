Fin-Leasing Process Category

1. FOH-Fin-Leasing-Create-Leads

1.1 Fellow complete testdrive process

Here we would like to deffinate the standard process for the online and offline process for the test drive. It starts with the first contact of the user and ends with the follow-up after the test drive. The process is defined in such a way that a permanent qualitative consultation of our users can be guaranteed and all steps are as efficient as possible.

1.2 Fellow manually books testdrive for user

If the user contacts a fellow directly or doesn't know how to book a testdrive online, the local fellow should propose to manually book testdrive for the users. The fellow then goes to Titan and books the testdrive manually.

1.3 Fellow handles a testdrive cancelation or change request

If the user contacts fellow to change testdive location, date or to cancel test drive, the fellow can go to Titan and perform the action for the users.

1.4 Fellow testdrive car preparation

Fellow prepares the car, checks testdrive agreement and

1.5 Fellow follows up testdrive leads

After the testdrive, fellow consults with the user about his experience and catches potential leads

1.6 Fellow fills damage form together with users

The car is damaged after the testdrive and fellow needs to fill out a form together with the user
